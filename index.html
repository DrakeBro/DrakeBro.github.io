I'm Drake Alexander Broer, a B.S. Information Systems student at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (Dean's List). I currently work as a Student IT Consultant at UMBC's Department of Information Technology, providing front-line support for students and staff across hardware, software, networking, and account issues.

Alongside IT support, I serve as Lead Teaching Assistant for UMBC's Introduction to Computer Programming course — coordinating six TAs, leading office hours, and helping ~30 students build real programming skills each semester.

My technical foundation spans Python, Java, SQL, Linux, and Bash, with additional experience in tools like WireShark, Request Tracker, and PowerBI. I'm also Secretary of the Information Systems Council of Majors and an active member of the Information Systems Security Association.
